Frequently Asked Questions

How does name matching work?

Ball IQ uses smart matching—you don't need to spell names perfectly. "Lebron" matches "LeBron James", and common nicknames work too. If you're close enough, it counts!

Why wasn't my answer accepted?

Our database covers players from recent decades. Very old historical players or minor league callups might not be included. If you think we're missing someone, let us know!

How do I play online multiplayer?

Select "Online VS" mode, and you'll get a 4-letter room code. Share this code with your friend—they enter it to join your game. Both players need an internet connection.

Can I delete my data?

Your game data (scores, records) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. For online multiplayer data, contact us and we'll delete it.

Is Ball IQ free?

Ball IQ is free to download and play. We may add optional premium features in the future, but the core game will always be free.

I found a bug / have a suggestion

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at jameswrobel25@gmail.com with details. Bug reports with screenshots are especially helpful.
