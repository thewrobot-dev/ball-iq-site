Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

The short version: Ball IQ is a trivia game. We collect minimal data to make the game work. We don't sell your information. Ever.

What We Collect

Ball IQ collects only what's necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Game Data: Your scores, records, and game progress are stored locally on your device and synced to our servers for online multiplayer features.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers to support multiplayer matchmaking and prevent abuse.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how the app is used to help us improve the game (via Firebase Analytics).

What We Don't Collect

  • Your real name or personal identity
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your location (GPS)
  • Your photos or files
  • Any payment information (handled by Apple/Google)

How We Use Your Data

  • To save your game progress and personal bests
  • To enable online multiplayer features
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To fix bugs and crashes

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), which employs industry-standard security measures. Game data is associated with anonymous identifiers, not your personal identity.

Third-Party Services

Ball IQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Database, analytics, and authentication — Privacy Policy
  • Expo: App framework and updates — Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Ball IQ is a general audience app rated 4+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to play.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the app
  • Request deletion of any server-side data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling it in your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
